# Voice — Newsletter / Governance Digest

Extends `voice-core.md`. Use for the periodic newsletter, governance digest, and broadcast emails to the dev list.

## 1. Subject lines
- 4–7 words. State the thing; don't tease.
- Sample (good): *"This week in v4 hooks: 12 new deployments"*
- Sample (avoid): *"You won't believe what shipped last week"*

## 2. Preview text
- Extends the subject; never repeats it.
- Sample: *"Plus: governance proposal #67 enters the voting period."*

## 3. Body cadence
- Open with the most consequential thing first. No "hi everyone", no "we hope you're well".
- Sample opener: *"Proposal 67 entered voting yesterday. Here's what it does and who's already weighed in."*
- Paragraphs: 1–3 sentences.
- One primary link per section, surfaced inline.

## 4. Section grammar (newsletter)
- *What shipped* (links to PRs / docs)
- *Governance this week* (proposals + delegate posts)
- *Things to read* (one or two outside links)
- *Builder's corner* (one ICP-1 item per issue)

## 5. Closing
- No "Cheers,". No "Best,".
- Signed by team handle, e.g. *— uniswap fdn team*. Never an individual name unless that person genuinely wrote the issue.
- No PS unless the PS contains the actual primary action.

## 6. Length
- Weekly newsletter: 250–450 words.
- Governance digest: 350–600 words (proposals are dense).
- Dev broadcast: under 150 words.
