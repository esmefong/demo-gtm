# Uniswap — Brand Kit (demo outline)

Visual and naming standards for this demo repo. The real Uniswap brand kit lives in the public Uniswap Foundation repo and the Uniswap Labs design system; this is a stripped sample.

## 1. Palette
- **Primary:** Uniswap Pink (the iconic 🦄 hue — used for headlines, primary CTAs, and brand lockups)
- **Accent:** Black / off-white for type. Mint and yellow used sparingly for category tagging.
- **Neutrals:** A small set of greys for body and dividers.
- *(Hex values, accessibility ratios, and dark/light pair behaviour live in the design system, not this file.)*

## 2. Typography
- **Display:** a geometric sans for headlines.
- **Body:** the same family at lighter weight.
- **Mono:** for numbers, addresses, and code in product UI screenshots.

## 3. Naming
- The protocol is **Uniswap**. The flagship versions are **Uniswap v2 / v3 / v4**.
- The aggregator product is **UniswapX**.
- The token is **UNI** (always all-caps).
- The non-profit is the **Uniswap Foundation**. The company is **Uniswap Labs**. They are not interchangeable; pick the right one.
- Features are lowercase common nouns: *hooks*, *flash accounting*, *singleton pool*, *governance proposal*.

## 4. Tagline
- This demo does not generate new taglines. Existing public-facing one-liners are owned by the Foundation and Labs respectively. Use them verbatim or not at all.

## 5. Iconography
- 🦄 is reserved. Do not pepper it through copy. Use it in lockups and at most once per piece of social copy, only when structurally meaningful.
- Logo always paired with the wordmark in marketing.
- Minimum clear space: 1× the height of the "U".
