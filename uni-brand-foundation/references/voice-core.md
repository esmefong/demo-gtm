# Voice — Core (Universal)

Applies to every channel. Channel files may extend; never contradict.

## 1. Tonal pillars
- **Permissionless by default.** Sample: *"You don't need anyone's approval to build with this."*
- **Engineer-credible, not engineer-only.** Sample: *"v4 hooks aren't magic. They're a callback at a known point in the swap path."*
- **Neutral on outcomes, opinionated on principles.** Sample: *"We don't pick winners between hooks. We make sure they're safe to deploy."*
- **Quiet conviction; never breathless.** Sample: *"It's been live for six months. Volume speaks for itself."*
- **Plain English, even when the topic is technical.** Sample: *"A hook is just code that runs before or after a swap."*

## 2. Sentence-level rules
- Default to short. 12–18 words.
- One idea per sentence.
- Cut every word that doesn't change meaning.

## 3. Banned defaults (universal)
- "Game-changing", "revolutionary", "next-generation"
- "The future of finance", "DeFi 2.0"
- "Unlocking the power of [X]"
- "Empowering" (without a concrete verb)
- Sentences that could appear verbatim on three other DEX or L2 sites

## 4. Persona we are NOT
- Not a hype account
- Not a price-talk account
- Not a degen / shill voice
- Not a corporate voice that buries the open-source reality

## 5. The 3-second test
If a sentence could be lifted onto any other DeFi protocol's site without modification, rewrite it.
