# Uniswap — ICPs (demo)

Two primary ICPs for this demo. Every piece of content must target exactly one.

## ICP 1 — The v4 hook developer
- **Title / role:** smart-contract engineer at a protocol team, a quant fund building on-chain, or an independent dev shipping their own dApp.
- **Pain:** v4 hooks unlock a lot, but the canonical examples are still thin. Audit costs are real. They want to ship something nobody has.
- **Belief:** *"The pool is fine. The interesting work happens at the seams."*
- **Job to be done:** Ship a novel hook to mainnet without burning weeks on infra plumbing.
- **Vocabulary they use:** *singleton, beforeSwap, afterSwap, flash accounting, hooks, salt, Foundry, anvil, deploy script, fork test, EIP-1153, ERC-6909.*
- **Vocabulary they DON'T use:** *DeFi 2.0, ecosystem play, leverage (verb), unlock the power of.*

## ICP 2 — The DAO treasury manager / institutional allocator
- **Title / role:** treasury lead at a DAO, head of digital assets at a fund, on-chain treasurer at a protocol foundation, or a multisig signer at an org with $5M–$300M in stables and tokens.
- **Pain:** Treasury sits in stables earning ~5% in T-bill ETFs while the org's mission is on-chain. Boards ask why. Compliance asks how.
- **Belief:** *"On-chain liquidity should outperform a money-market fund eventually — but not at the cost of headline risk."*
- **Job to be done:** Deploy a portion of treasury into liquidity positions or yield-bearing strategies that survive a board review and a 4-hour incident.
- **Vocabulary they use:** *TVL, fee tier, IL, slippage, MEV, custody, multisig, attestation, exposure, drawdown, ops runbook.*
- **Vocabulary they DON'T use:** *yield farming, ape into, alpha leak, looksrare.*

## Cross-ICP rule
- Name the ICP in frontmatter before drafting.
- Use only the vocabulary that ICP uses. Different language for ICP-1 vs ICP-2 is a feature.
- If a draft would speak to both ICPs equally, it speaks to neither. Pick one.
