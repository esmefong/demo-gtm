# Voice — Landing Page

Extends `voice-core.md`. Use for product, docs landing, hackathon, hook-spotlight, and campaign pages.

## 1. Above the fold
- **H1:** what it is + who it's for. Sample: *"Hooks turn Uniswap v4 into a programmable liquidity layer for builders."*
- **Subhead:** the non-obvious mechanism or outcome. Sample: *"Run any logic — TWAMM, dynamic fees, on-chain limit orders — at the moment of a swap."*
- **Primary CTA:** verb + outcome. Sample: *"Read the hook walkthrough"* / *"Deploy a hook on testnet"*.

## 2. Section grammar
Each section follows: claim → evidence → friction it removes.
- Sample claim: *"Hooks compose with the Uniswap pool you already trust."*
- Sample evidence: *"The same singleton, the same accounting, with your logic at the seams."*
- Sample friction: *"No new pool factory. No new audit of the core."*

## 3. Social proof
- Specific numbers, named integrations.
- Sample: *"Used in production by 14 hook builders shipping over $40M in TVL across hooks."*
- Logo walls only when genuinely warranted; numbers preferred.

## 4. Forbidden landing-page tropes
- "The all-in-one DeFi platform"
- "Built for the next generation of finance"
- "Trusted by leaders in DeFi"
- Hero rendering of a generic chart, dashboard, or unicorn-as-decoration

## 5. CTA hierarchy
- One primary action.
- One secondary (usually "read the docs" or "view the GitHub").
- No third CTA.
