# Voice — LinkedIn

Extends `voice-core.md`. Use for institutional positioning, regulatory updates, hiring, and content aimed at TradFi-adjacent readers who don't live on X.

## 1. Post structure
- **Hook (line 1):** institution-credible, specific, never crypto-meme.
- **Setup (lines 2–4):** the operational reality the reader recognizes.
- **The point:** one stance or one observation.
- **Proof:** numbers, named partners, or governance-forum links.
- **Close:** a question or a stance — not a CTA pile.

## 2. Length targets
- Default: 100–180 words. Hard ceiling: 220.

## 3. Hook patterns we use
- **The compliance-aware framing.** Sample: *"A DAO treasury earning yield isn't a regulatory question yet. It will be."*
- **The infra framing.** Sample: *"The same protocol that retail uses to swap $50 also clears 8-figure trades."*
- **The hiring/ops framing.** Sample: *"We're hiring a head of legal. The job is harder than it looks."*

## 4. Hook patterns we avoid
- Anything beginning "🚀" or "🦄"
- "I'm thrilled to announce..."
- "Hot take:" openers
- Anything that requires the reader to already be on Crypto Twitter

## 5. Formatting
- Line breaks between every 1–2 sentences.
- Zero emojis in posts targeting institutional readers.
- Two relevant tags max, at the end. Never #DeFi alone — too broad.
